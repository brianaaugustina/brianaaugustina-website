Bio

Briana Ottoboni is a San Francisco-based founder and creative who spent years growing brands and startups before deciding to build something of her own.

After apprenticing with a shoe cobbler as the world became more automated, The Trades Show was born as a storytelling medium to highlight artisan pathways in a return to what it means to be human. Since storytelling alone can't sustain an entire industry, she built The Corral next, infrastructure to power the next generation of craftworkers.

Briana operates at the intersection of craft, culture, and technology, creating the stories, tools, and platforms for a more human life.
